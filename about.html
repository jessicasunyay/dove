our mission

we strive to comfort and create a safe haven for students feeling overwhelmed and stressed.

the issue at root

as the covid-19 pandemic set in, montgomery county public schools rushed to put together an online learning plan. however, this failed to take into account the thousands of students feeling constantly overwhelmed, anxious, and stressed during virtual school.

how dove helps

to provide solvency for this crisis, we offer an interactive website which enables students feeling overwhelmed, during or outside of class. students can embark on different journeys based on what they feel they may be struggling with, or can browse around to launch towards a better lifestyle.
